How to use the Inventory System

Adding data

Insert the product name and product quantity. Click on ADD button to save the product into the inventory system.
Please note that both product name and product quantity are required.

Finding data

Insert the product name to search for the data. Click on FIND button to search for the product from the inventory system.

Deleting data

Insert the product name. Click on DELETE button to delete the product from the inventory system.
